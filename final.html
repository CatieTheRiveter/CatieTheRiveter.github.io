Sweet Times Cupcakes and Coffee

We start out every morning with the highest quality ingredients that we can find; we blend together Madagascar Bourbon vanilla beans, real Belgian chocolate, pure extracts, and seasonal fruits to create our scrumptious cupcakes, silky ganaches and creamy Italian buttercreams. Our handcrafted babycakes are made without preservatives and baked in our store in small batches throughout the day to ensure they are at their finest. Try them with a cup of our Sweet Times Blend grinded-and-made-to-order coffee.

Sweet Times Coffee

We thoughtfully selected the perfect combination of 100% Arabica coffee beans, that are locally roasted in small batches to bring you the freshest coffee available. At Sweet Times we want to ensure our coffee maintains its distinctive flavor, rich body and amazing aroma by grinding and making every single cup to order so you can taste our best in every sip.
